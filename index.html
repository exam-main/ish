AI X-ray Interpretation System

AI-assisted interpretation of radiographic images

This platform demonstrates the use of artificial intelligence to assist in the interpretation of X-ray images. The system provides descriptive findings based on visual patterns commonly associated with radiological conditions.

Pulmonary Pneumonia

Increased radiopacity and focal infiltrates observed within lung fields, commonly associated with inflammatory processes.

Pulmonary Tuberculosis

Presence of cavitary lesions, fibrotic changes, and nodular opacities predominantly in upper lung zones.

Skeletal Fractures

Disruption of cortical bone continuity, including complete fractures or micro-fracture patterns.

Pulmonary Edema

Diffuse bilateral opacities indicative of interstitial or alveolar fluid accumulation.

Cardiomegaly

Enlargement of cardiac silhouette exceeding normal cardiothoracic ratio.
